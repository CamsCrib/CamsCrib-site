Even More About Me

One of the biggest things I enjoy is Outer Space. I enjoying simulating custom planets on Universe Sandbox.

This planet is called Menerva and it orbits a G-class star, similar to our Sun. Menerva is a Earth-like planet meaning it is rocky and containing around a similar size to Earth. It contains two silicate moons that don't have any names at the moment. The are Volcanic moons because of the tidal forces that are present from Menerva and they also tug on each other. Menerva's atmosphere contains mainly water vapor and small amounts of carbon dioxide and methane. Which means the atmosphere is pretty thick. It's poles contain liquid water and you could assume that the storms on this planet would be pretty bad. Enjoy the view of the planet and it's moons!
